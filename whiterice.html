White Rice

Description

The general rule is that the shorter the rice grain, the stickier the rice. Sticky doesn’t meany gummy! It just means the grains stick together more, rather than being crumbly, so they are easier to eat with chopsticks.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 rice

Steps

  1. Step 1: Place 1 cup of rice (no rinsing!) and 1.5 cups water in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium high – no lid;
  2. Step 2: Turn heat down to low, cover with lid, and leave for 13 minutes;
  3. Step 3: With lid still on, remove saucepan from stove and stand for 10 minutes; and
  4. Fluff with a rice paddle, rubber spatula or fork, marvel at perfect fluffy rice! {See recipe video for a good close up!}
